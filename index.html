what black panthers eat

the diet of about 70% of black Panthers is deer tapir wild hogs and raccoons baby black panthers drink milk from there mother when they are babys as they grow older they start eating other small animals like lizards and rabbits

what do they do black panthers look like?

these melanistic panthers have comparatively smaller heads large bodies and long tails in reality black panthers have a spotted pattern but they tend to be hidden due to their dark coat black panthers eyes are emerald-colored and glow in the night
